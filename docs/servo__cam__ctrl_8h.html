Detailed Description

Digital Camera Control.

Provides the control of the shutter and the zoom of a digital camera through standard binary IOs of the board.

Configuration: Since the API of led.h is used, connected pins must be defined as led numbers (usually in the airframe file): <define name="DC_SHUTTER_SERVO" value="6"> <define name="DC_ZOOM_IN_SERVO" value="7"> <define name="DC_ZOOM_OUT_SERVO" value="8"> <define name="DC_POWER_SERVO" value="9"> Related bank and pin must also be defined: <define name="LED_6_BANK" value="0"> <define name="LED_6_PIN" value="2"> The required initialization (dc_init()) and periodic (4Hz) process
